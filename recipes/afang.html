Afang Recipes

This is an outcome of Afang Soup

An African Soup originated from the country Nigeria

Below are the list of Recipe used

  • Pomo
  • Cray Fish
  • Stock Fish
  • Assorted Meat
  • Groundnut oil
  • Blended Pepper
  • Red oil
  • Seasoning
  • Vegetable
  • Salt
  1. Add Your red and Groundnut to the pot on Fire
  2. Let it heat for 5 minute
  3. Add your blended Pepper
  4. Add your seasonings
  5. Add your meat and fish
  6. Wait for 10 - 15 minute
  7. Add your vegetable
